About Tulku Dakpa Rinpoche

Tulku Dakpa Rinpoche was recognized by His Holiness Mindrolling Trichen Rinpoche as a reincarnation of Drupwang Rogza Sonam Palge. As the founder and spiritual director of the Danakosha Dharma Center, he brings ancient teachings to modern audiences through innovative practices and a commitment to compassion.

Learn about his journey, teachings, and the transformative experience he offers.
